Oceanic Institute of Hawaii VR Experience

Dive into the fascinating world of oceanic research with our immersive and interactive VR project, specifically designed for the Oceanic Institute of Hawaii. Explore the institute and its surroundings through a lifelike 360-degree video experience, and engage in various activities with our interactive 3D environments. Discover the groundbreaking work being done by the institute and learn about marine life, conservation efforts, and much more.

Introduction

Welcome to the Oceanic Institute of Hawaii VR Experience! This unique project has been developed in collaboration with the Oceanic Institute of Hawaii to provide an unparalleled opportunity for users to explore the institute's premises and gain insights into the valuable work being done in marine research and conservation. Utilizing cutting-edge technology, this VR experience brings you closer than ever to the wonders of the ocean and the dedicated scientists working to protect it.

Key Features

  • 360-Degree Video: Our VR experience leverages high-quality 360-degree footage captured with the Insta360 Pro 2 camera, allowing users to feel as if they are physically present at the institute and its surroundings.
  • Interactive 3D Environments: Dive into meticulously crafted 3D environments that recreate various on-site activities, offering users a hands-on approach to learning about the institute's work.
  • Built on Unity: This project has been developed using Unity, a robust and flexible platform that allows for seamless integration of 360-degree video and interactive 3D content.
  • Meta Quest and Quest Pro Compatibility: The Oceanic Institute of Hawaii VR Experience has been designed to run on both Meta Quest and Quest Pro devices, ensuring an accessible and high-quality experience for all users.
  • Computer Required: To enjoy the full potential of this immersive experience, users will need a compatible computer to maintain and edit the application.

Technical Details

  • Insta360 Pro 2 Camera: Our team used the Insta360 Pro 2 camera to capture stunning, high-resolution 360-degree video content for the project.
  • Custom C# Scripts: We developed numerous C# scripts to handle the various interactions and transitions between interactive scenes, creating a smooth and engaging experience for users.
  • Platform: Unity
  • Compatible Devices: Meta Quest and Quest Pro

Conclusion

The Oceanic Institute of Hawaii VR Experience is an innovative project that invites users to explore the world of oceanic research in an immersive and interactive way. By providing a realistic sense of presence and engaging activities, we hope to increase public awareness and understanding of the vital work being done by the Oceanic Institute of Hawaii. Join us on this virtual journey, and experience the depths of the ocean like never before!

My Contributions to the Project

As one of the developer of this project, I played a pivotal role in bringing the Oceanic Institute of Hawaii VR Experience to life. My responsibilities and accomplishments include:

  • On-Site Video Recording: I personally visited the Oceanic Institute of Hawaii and captured the 360-degree video footage using the Insta360 Pro 2 camera. This involved careful planning and execution to ensure that the final product would be as immersive and realistic as possible.
  • Video Processing: After capturing the footage, I processed the videos using the Insta360 Stitcher software to create seamless, high-quality 360-degree videos suitable for the VR experience.
  • Importing Videos into Unity: I imported the processed videos into the Unity platform and integrated them with the 3D environments, ensuring smooth transitions and a cohesive overall experience.
  • Scripting: I wrote custom C# scripts to manage the tracking of scene information and transition data. These scripts facilitate seamless navigation through the experience and enable user interactions with the 3D environment.
